🛡️ Mercenaries
Mercs are hired from Guardian NPCs in PoK (near each racial gate and along the wall by Castle Drewe). Right-click a Guardian to open the merc hire window. Mercs are free on this server (purchase/upkeep costs disabled).

Merc types: Tank (Warrior) and Healer (Cleric)
Tiers: Apprentice (I-V) and Journeyman (I-V) — Journeyman are significantly better
Proficiencies: Each race offers different merc options

Mercs are controlled through the Mercenary window (default hotkey: Alt+M or through the EQ menu). No chat commands needed — everything is done through the UI window.
Hire a Merc
Right-click any Guardian NPC in PoK → Select merc type/tier → Hire
Suspend Merc
Use Mercenary window → Suspend button. Saves merc for later resummon.
Unsuspend Merc
Use Mercenary window → Unsuspend button. Brings merc back.
Change Stance
Mercenary window → Stance dropdown. Controls combat behavior.
Dismiss Merc
Mercenary window → Dismiss button. Permanently releases the merc.
Note: If Guardian NPCs aren't showing in PoK, run #repop poknowledge in-game after zoning to PoK. Their spawn expansion requirement has been set to always spawn regardless of server expansion.
🗺️ EC Tunnel Hub — Custom NPCs
All custom NPCs are located in East Commonlands (ecommons) near the tunnel entrance at y=-1519. Line up from west to east: Bubar → Shady Sam → Lady Luck → Brother Kail → Master Rashan → Gear Wrangler Vex.
Shady Sam Casino
Casino NPC. Bet platinum and try your luck. Win big and get a clicky jackpot item.
Say: bet 10  |  bet 100  |  bet 1000
Say: odds  |  jackpot
Lady Luck Grab Bag
Item grab bag NPC. Pay platinum, receive a class-appropriate item. Higher bets = better odds. Items are chosen for your class (monk, caster, priest, melee).
Say: 10 plat  |  100 plat  |  1000 plat  |  5000 plat
Say: odds
10pp60% Common / 28% Rare / 10% Legendary / 2% Fabled
100pp20% / 48% / 25% / 7%
1000pp5% / 25% / 50% / 20%
5000pp0% / 10% / 45% / 45%
⚠️ Money display updates on zone — deduction happens immediately server-side.
Brother Kail Monk Trainer
Monk trainer NPC. Right-click or use /train to train monk skills. AllowCrossClassTrainers is enabled — any trainer works for any class.
/train  (while targeting Brother Kail)
Master Rashan Tome Vendor
Sells all monk discipline tomes — 77 tomes from classic to RoF2 era. Right-click to open merchant window. Tomes are priced by tier.
Right-click to browse & buy
50ppClassic discs (Whirlwind, Hundred Fists, Voiddance, Stone Stance…)
200ppMid-tier (Scaledfist, Impenetrable, Delay Death…)
500ppAdvanced (Drunken Monkey Style, Cloud of Fists, Crane Stance…)

Gear Wrangler Vex Bot Outfitter
Auto-equips ALL your bots with curated gear for their class and level (10-level bands, class-aware scoring). Also saves and restores YOUR personal gear set — great for recovering after a death.
Bot Outfitting Workflow:
  1. Camp each bot: ^botcamp (while each bot is targeted)
  2. Talk to Vex → say outfit bots
  3. Respawn each bot: ^botspawn BotName
Player Gear Save/Restore:
  1. While fully geared: say save gear — snapshots all worn slots to DB
  2. After dying: say restore gear — re-summons every saved item to your cursor
  3. Say what's saved to review your saved set slot by slot
Say: outfit bots   — equip ALL your bots
Say: outfit [BotName]  — equip one specific bot
Say: list   — see your bots and their class/level
Say: save gear   — save YOUR current worn gear
Say: restore gear  — re-summon your saved gear after death
Say: what's saved  — list your saved gear set
Find Best Item for a Slot:
Queries the DB live for the best item for your exact class & level. Summons it to your cursor.
Say: find [slot]  — e.g. find head, find chest, find primary
Say: find primary [type]  — weapon subtype: find primary 1hb, find primary h2h, find primary 1hs
Say: find ammo  — best arrow/bolt (WAR PAL RNG SHD ROG only)
Say: find power  — best power source
Say: find range  — best ranged weapon
Say: slots  — list all valid slot names
Epic Weapons:
Summons your class epic directly to your cursor. All 16 classes supported.
Say: find epic1.0  — summon your Epic 1.0 weapon
Say: find epic2.0  — summon your Epic 2.0 weapon
⚠️ Bots must be camped before outfitting, then respawned after to load new gear.

🔧 Tips & Tricks
Press ~ to open MQ2 chat window — type commands there or in normal chat
MQ2 commands work in the normal EQ chat box too — just prefix with / as usual
/stick is great for melee — target a mob and /stick to auto-follow it in combat
Use /mapfilter npc on and /mapfilter npc_corpse off to only show living NPCs
MQ2Map shows your exact coordinates — great with #goto GM commands
If MQ2 didn't inject, check that MacroQuest2.exe launched — look for it in Task Manager via Proton
/mqpause pauses macro execution without ending it
The Macros/setup.mac included can auto-equip and buff your character on login
