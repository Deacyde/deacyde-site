🪧 Admin Signs v0.1.0
Glowing wall signs that trigger admin actions when right-clicked by any player. Place them in lobbies, spawn areas, or admin rooms. Signs can toggle gamemode, set time/weather, heal, give items, toggle fly, or give a Nether kit.
🎁 Give Signs
💡 How Each Sign Works
Gamemode Sign — Right-click cycles the clicker through Survival → Creative → Spectator → Adventure.
Time Sign — Right-click toggles between day and night.
Weather Sign — Right-click cycles Clear → Rain → Thunder.
Heal Sign — Right-click fully restores health and hunger of the clicker.
Give Sign — Right-click gives the clicker a preset item bundle (configure in sign NBT).
Fly Sign — Right-click toggles flight ability for the clicker.
Nether Kit Sign — Right-click gives fire resistance, a full set of armor, and weapons for Nether survival.
📋 Usage Notes
Place the sign block like any other block — it attaches to a wall.
Signs are designed for OP/admin rooms — any player who right-clicks them will get the effect.
To remove, break the sign with any tool (or your hand).
⚙️ Conveyor Belts v1.1.0
Full conveyor belt system with straight, corner, slope, vertical belts at three speeds, plus splitters, mergers, detector belts, conveyor chests, vacuum plates, belt hoppers, and belt droppers. Use the Wrench to adjust direction.
🔧 Tools
🎁 Give Belts — Normal Speed
🎁 Give Belts — Fast
🎁 Give Belts — Slow
🎁 Give Special Blocks
💡 Tips
Wrench — Right-click a belt to rotate it 90°. Shift+right-click a corner belt to flip its turn direction.
Splitter Belt — Distributes items to left and right alternately.
Merger Belt — Combines two belt lines into one.
Detector Belt — Emits a redstone signal when an item passes over it.
Conveyor Chest — A chest that auto-inserts items into adjacent belts.
Vacuum Plate — A pressure plate that sucks nearby dropped items onto the belt.
Belt Hopper — Pulls items from a chest above and places them on the belt.
Belt Dropper — Drops items from the belt into an inventory below.
Tube Exit — Endpoint for item transport tubes; items exit here onto a belt.
Slope Belts — Up/down variants move items vertically between floors.
📐 Schem Builder v0.1.0
Load and place .schem files in-world with preview, rotation, and incremental tick-based placement. Works with the Minecraft Build Studio app. Drop schematics in the server's schembuilder/schematics/ folder.
🎁 Give Items
📋 Workflow
Step 1 — Add schematic: Place your .schem file into the server folder schembuilder/schematics/. Files placed here are available to the Schematic Placer.
Step 2 — Get the Schematic Placer: /give @s schembuilder:schematic_placer — Right-click to open a GUI listing all available schematics.
Step 3 — Select & preview: Choose a schematic from the list. A ghost preview appears at your cursor position.
Step 4 — Rotate: Scroll wheel or a hotkey rotates the preview in 90° increments before placing.
Step 5 — Place: Left-click to confirm placement. Large schematics are placed incrementally over multiple ticks to avoid lag.
Schematic Copier: Right-click two corners of an existing build to capture it as a .schem file saved to the schematics folder.
⚙️ Config (server-side)
placement.maxBlocks — Max blocks placed per operation (default: 50,000).
placement.maxDimension — Max schematic dimension in blocks (default: 128).
placement.blocksPerTick — How many blocks are placed per tick (default: 200).
placement.cooldownTicks — Ticks between placements per player (default: 200).
permissions.requiredOpLevel — Minimum OP level to use the placer (default: 2).
files.folder — Schematics folder path relative to server root (default: schembuilder/schematics).
🔫 Mechanical Defense Turrets v2.5.0
17 mechanical defense turrets with targeting AI, internal ammo storage, overheat system, and configurable behavior. Place facing any direction. Right-click to open the turret GUI and load ammo.
🎁 Give Turrets
💡 Tips
Arrow Turret — Standard ranged turret. Loads regular arrows.
Fire Arrow Turret — Fires flaming arrows. Loads arrows + flint & steel.
Snowball Turret — Knockback projectiles. Loads snowballs.
TNT Turret — Launches lit TNT. High damage radius. Loads TNT blocks.
Splash Potion Turret — Fires any splash potion. Loads splash potions.
Lightning Turret — Calls lightning strikes on targets. No ammo needed.
Laser Turret — High-speed beam attack. No ammo needed.
Fireball Turret — Ghast-style fireball projectiles. No ammo needed.
Wither Skull Turret — Fires wither skulls applying the Wither effect.
Trident Turret — Throws tridents. Loads tridents.
Ender Turret — Teleports enemies randomly. No ammo needed.
Shulker Turret — Fires shulker bullets that apply levitation. No ammo needed.
Cobweb Turret — Entangles enemies in cobwebs. Loads string/cobwebs.
Knockback Turret — Massive knockback force. No ammo needed.
Anvil Turret — Drops anvils on targets. Loads anvils.
Dragon Breath Turret — Area-of-effect lingering damage cloud. Loads dragon's breath.
Ice Turret — Freezes targets in place. No ammo needed.
📋 General Notes
Turrets target hostile mobs by default. Right-click GUI to configure targeting: mobs, players, or both.
Each turret has an overheat bar — rapid firing causes cooldown until it cools.
Turrets track facing direction when placed — place while looking in the direction you want coverage.
Break a turret to recover it (loot drops per the mod's loot table).
🛸 UFO Mod v0.1.0
Adds a flyable UFO vehicle with weapons, a tractor beam, and internal inventory. Craft or spawn the UFO Item, then right-click to board. Use keybinds to fire weapons and toggle hover mode.
🎁 Give & Summon
⌨️ Keybinds
Toggle Hover / Fly Modeconfigurable
Cycle Weaponconfigurable
Fire Weaponconfigurable
Set keybinds in Options → Controls → UFO Mod category.
💡 Tips
Board the UFO: Right-click the UFO entity to enter. Right-click again (or press Sneak) to exit.
Hover Mode: Toggle hover to stay at a fixed altitude — easier for aiming weapons and using the tractor beam.
Tractor Beam: Lifts mobs and items beneath the UFO. Useful for moving animals or trolling players.
Internal Inventory: The UFO has storage accessible while piloting — useful for collected items.
Spawning via command: Use /summon ufomod:ufo ~ ~ ~ to spawn a UFO entity at your location without the item.
