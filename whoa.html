Beelink SSH Setup

This page contains the public key and exact steps to let Copilot access the Beelink again. The public key is safe to share. Do not share the private key.

1. Public key to copy

ssh-ed25519 AAAAC3NzaC1lZDI1NTE5AAAAII2C9tiey7SINMNIpiJwWKw/UNDsBb19b2+m6ATmubLG beelink-cli@steamdeck

2. On the Beelink, open PowerShell as Administrator and paste this whole block

Set-Service sshd -StartupType Automatic
Start-Service sshd
@'
ssh-ed25519 AAAAC3NzaC1lZDI1NTE5AAAAII2C9tiey7SINMNIpiJwWKw/UNDsBb19b2+m6ATmubLG beelink-cli@steamdeck
'@ | Set-Content "C:\ProgramData\ssh\administrators_authorized_keys"
icacls "C:\ProgramData\ssh\administrators_authorized_keys" /inheritance:r
icacls "C:\ProgramData\ssh\administrators_authorized_keys" /grant "SYSTEM:F"
icacls "C:\ProgramData\ssh\administrators_authorized_keys" /grant "Administrators:F"
Restart-Service sshd

3. Then come back here

After that is done, tell Copilot done and it will test SSH and fix the Beelink status service.
